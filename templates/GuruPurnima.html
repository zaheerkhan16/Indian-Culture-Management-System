Guru Purnima Festival

Guru Purnima is a spiritual tradition that celebrates the importance of gurus, both spiritual and academic, in one's life. It is a day to express deep gratitude and seek blessings from these guiding figures.

Devotees visit temples and offer flowers, fruits, and sweets to their gurus as a symbol of respect. It is a time for reflection on the significance of knowledge, wisdom, and the influence of mentors in our lives.
